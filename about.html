About Us



In many countries that face massive development shortfalls, social progress is slowed by concentration of (passive) wealth in few minorities, insufficient government aid, and fragmentation of existing humanitarian or development initiatives.

Pravaha stands for ‘flow’ – a conduit channeling pooled capital, resources, and capabilities, towards perceptible social development.

Pravaha wants to reimagine “philanthropy” and social responsibility from a closed, opaque and fragmented individual efforts to an open and collaborative paradigm, bringing together multiple sources of capital (from individuals, corporates, existing donors, and more) to tackle major development challenges in emerging economies. Pravaha works with donors, professionals, other philanthropic organizations and the government, with an emphasis on co-creation and result- centric partnerships.

Just as private, for-profit investment vehicles have achieved tremendous scale through pooling capital to address visionary changes of the next decades, so too do we believe that the development sector needs such an open, scaled, and impactful entity.

Pravaha’s major focus in the long-term includes poverty and inequality, education, management and social investment / spending, social innovation, and children and young people.
