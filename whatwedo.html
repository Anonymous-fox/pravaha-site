What We Do

Pravaha is reimagining “philanthropy.” Championing openness and collaboration, Pravaha aims to bring various stakeholders together to tackle development issues at scale. Our mission is to serve as a broad, inclusive platform for result-centric partnerships between existing NGOs, the government, and donors.

Pravaha sees education as the vital component that spurs social growth, and catalyzes individual equity and effective participation in society. Education is particularly relevant in the current social circumstances since it is a central pillar of the equality paradigm. Education is both a way of reducing obstacles to the full exercise of the right to education, and for achieving progress in multiple dimensions of social inclusion. Pravaha works in the education field to provide greater equality of opportunity, skills for future social mobility, and the formation of an active citizenry respectful of rights and duties. One of our current projects is Prajna Academy, a school for gifted and talented children.

Pravaha recognizes existing challenges that healthcare systems have to face in order to deal with the most pressing health needs at the grassroots level. Health needs arising from the country’s demographic transitions, urbanization, and large population renders the current healthcare infrastructure unable to service core needs across the populace. In order to champion new healthcare models and coverage, Pravaha focuses on finding bespoke alternatives to the problems of unequal access to healthcare. These projects connect policy intent and practice effectively, to bring health service coverage that includes both prevention and treatment. This includes strategies for improving access to medication, and evaluation for the incorporation and rational use of technology in healthcare.

The lack of basic sanitation facilities and water problems in both rural and urban areas in India today are outpacing our ability to devise solutions. Pravaha looks to connect the dots between water, sanitation, and health, with the aim of reducing poverty and inequality, generating jobs, and minimizing the risks of disease, pollution, and environmental stress. Our projects in this area contribute to improving the quality of life in underdeveloped areas, through the implementation of environmentally sustainable methods and technologies, providing drinking water consumption and adequate sanitation systems. Apart from building infrastructure capacities, the projects also emphasize socio-political comprehension, acceptance, and support.

Pravaha believes that sports can be utilized as a means for inspiring far reaching social development initiatives. Our projects are aimed at reaching and retaining young people in programs with broader objectives including employability, skills for life, education, health and well-being, and social and gender inclusion. By using sports as a catalyst to improving physical and mental health, discouraging substance abuse, and inspiring athletic as well as academic achievement, Pravaha focuses on a holistic uplift of the individual. These projects encourage social mobility, facilitate the workings of social acceptance, and strengthen society through their effect on social capital, trust, culture, and national pride.
