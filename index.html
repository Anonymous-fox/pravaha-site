About Us

In many countries that face massive development shortfalls, social progress is slowed by concentration of (passive) wealth in few minorities, insufficient government aid, and fragmentation of existing humanitarian or development initiatives.

Pravaha stands for ‘flow’ – a conduit channeling pooled capital, resources, and capabilities, towards perceptible social development.

What We Do

Pravaha is reimagining “philanthropy.” Championing openness and collaboration, Pravaha aims to bring various stakeholders together to tackle development issues at scale. Our mission is to serve as a broad, inclusive platform for result-centric partnerships between existing NGOs, the government, and donors.
